About Pitt to You

The 2019 Pitt to You program begins in June 2019 in China. International student mentees will be assigned a student ambassador who they will meet in either Beijing or Shanghai for a three-day visit including group activities, a welcome workshop, and a reception with recent alumni. Communication with the student ambassador will continue throughout the summer leading up to the start of the 2019-2020 academic year at Pitt. Through a combination of monthly group activities and individual meetings planned during the fall semester, the student ambassadors will mentor the new international students as they transition into campus life. The formal program concludes in December 2019.
